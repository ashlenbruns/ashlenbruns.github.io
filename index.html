About Me

If I could, I would forever be a student. After 11 years in various customer service positions, I have finally discovered a career path that allows me to be the “forever student” – coding! Having graduated with a degree in Studio Art and English, I have always gravitated toward combining the technical with the creative. Coding is the perfect balance of this – the languages are strict, but the applications are endless if you have the imagination to drive them forward. I am currently enrolled in the University of Minnesota Full-Stack Web Development Boot Camp. The pace is fast and there is always something new to learn, but I love the challenge and find great satisfaction in creating clean, working code.
